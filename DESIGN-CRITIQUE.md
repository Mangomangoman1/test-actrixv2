## Lowest-scoring section and improvement
The systems cards were the most likely to become ordinary. I added artifact treatments: a torn paper corner, red pin, monospaced labels, rotation/offset hover, and a wider inbox-cleanup row so it feels like a field board rather than generic service cards.

## One-level-deeper pass
Added visual language around paper, field notes, stamps, and margin annotations across the page so the motif carries through every section instead of only the hero.
